Emma Margaret Marie Tachard-Mackey, the spectacular French-British actress is professionally known as Emma Mackey. She starred in the famous Netflix comedy-drama series, “Sex Education” and played the role of Maeve Wiley. She won the hearts of the audience with her humor and adorable looks and reprised the same role in season 2 as well as season 3.

Early Life

Emma Mackey was born on January 4, 1996, in Le Mans, a City in Northwestern France on the Sarthe River, France. Her father, Philippe Mackey was a school headmaster, while her mother, Rachel Mackey, was a volunteer at a cause. Inputs about her siblings are not revealed by her yet. Her nationality is French-British because her father belongs to France and her mother is from Britain. She belongs to the Christian religion and has a White Caucasian descent ethnicity. She was raised in Sablé-sur-Sarthe and in 2013, she received her baccalauréat. Later, she moved to England to study English literature and language at the University of Seeds in West Yorkshire. She earned a degree in 2016.

Emma Mackey Career

In 2016, after graduating from the University, she made her debut in acting by playing the character of Michelle in the Television Show “Badger Lane”. Later in 2019, she worked in another Television show “Summit Fever” and appeared as Isabelle. In 2019, she landed a famous role as a quick-witted, business-savvy “bad girl” Maeve Wiley in the Netflix film “Sex Education”. She was featured in the main role where she convinces fellow student Otis Milburn for starting an underground sex therapy business at their school. In the same year, she did another short film titled “Tic” and charactered “Jess”. Recently, Mackey played the role of Holly in the 2020 film “The Winter Lake”.

In May 2020, it was announced that Emma will be starred Emily Brontë in a biopic that will emphasize her early life. She is set to appear in Kenneth Branagh’s upcoming mystery thriller film “Death on the Nile” which is planned to release on the 18th of December, 2020. Her other upcoming film “Eiffel” where she is portrayed as “Adrienne Bourgès” is to be released in the year 2021. Apart from the films, Mackey also appeared as a model for several fashion brands. In July 2017, she also did a photoshoot for AIDA Shoreditch’s summer collection.
