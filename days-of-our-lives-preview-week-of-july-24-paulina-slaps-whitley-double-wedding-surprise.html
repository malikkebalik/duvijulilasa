Days of Our Lives Preview: Week of July 24 Paulina Slaps Whitley Double Wedding Surprise

July 26, 2024
blog

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers preview video for the week of July 24-28 reveals that Stefan DiMera (Brandon Barash) will grin as he wishes Gabi Hernandez (Camila Banus) a happy wedding day. Gabi will marvel over the chance to marry her soulmate and count on it to be forever this time.

Of course, it wouldn’t be a Days wedding without a good dose of drama!

When Stefan tells Dimitri von Leuschner (Peter Porte) that he’s getting married this morning, Dimitri’s response will surprise him.

“What a coincidence…so am I,” Dimitri says with a sly smile in DOOL’s weekly preview video.

Stefan may lash out at Dimitri for trying to piggyback on his nuptials with Gabi, but he won’t let the DiMera interloper ruin his happiness.

Gabi and Stefan have waited a long time for this moment, so they’ll try to make the most of coming together to tie the knot again.

Meanwhile, it looks like Gwen Rizczech (Emily O’Brien) will try to soothe any pre-wedding jitters by telling herself that Dimitri only has eyes for her.

Gwen will count on Leo Stark (Greg Rikaart) to support her ahead of the marriage ceremony, but he’ll face some close calls with Dimitri along the way.

After Gwen sees some steamy text messages from Dimitri to Leo, she’ll definitely have questions.

Those questions may come at the worst possible time since the wedding will be on the verge of kicking off.

Other Days spoilers say Dr. Sorenson (Lisagaye Tomlinson) will want to do some genetic testing, so she’ll ask for EJ DiMera’s (Dan Feuerriegel) DNA sample during Nicole Walker’s (Arianne Zucker) upcoming appointment.

When Sloan Petersen (Jessica Serfaty) receives updates on that, she’ll worry this could lead to her getting busted.

Sloan used her own DNA sample for the paternity test, so she’ll fear this could all go horribly wrong.

“You tried to keep me from knowing that it’s my kid!” Eric shouts at Sloan in the weekly promo.

However, it looks like Sloan will just be imagining the worst. Days spoilers point to Sloan dreaming or daydreaming about Eric’s furious reaction, so she’ll be highly motivated to keep the truth under wraps.

Next, Paulina Price (Jackée Harry) will have an opportunity to confront Whitley King (Kim Coles) after she gets apprehended.

Whitley’s mental health issues are to blame for the Abe Carver (James Reynolds) kidnapping chaos, but Paulina clearly won’t have any sympathy.

Days of Our Lives spoilers say Paulina will slap Whitley across the face at the Salem PD, so she’ll let the former nurse know exactly what she thinks of her.
