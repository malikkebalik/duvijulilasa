General Hospital Spoilers: Ryan Stabs Austin Takes Over as Hook &amp; Strikes Avas New Man?

September 11, 2024
blog

General Hospital (GH) spoilers tease that there may soon be a new hook in town. Ryan Chamberlain (Jon Lindstrom) clearly thinks Heather Webber (Alley Mills) was incompetent during her own run as “The Hook,” so maybe it’s time for him to take over.

After all, Ryan was upset when he first heard the news about Ava Jerome’s (Maura West) attack. If anyone was going to hook Ava, Ryan felt it should be him!

It’s clear that Ryan has a dangerous obsession when it comes to Ava.

Ryan is possessive to the extreme, so that could lead to trouble – especially now that Austin Gatlin Holt (Roger Howarth) is in Ava’s orbit.

February sweeps spoilers insist that Austin will land in a deadly situation.

A case of terrible timing will put Austin’s life on the line, so that could be due to serial killer Ryan’s escape.

Ryan may assume Austin is Ava’s new lover and target him as a result.

That’s especially true if Austin plays the hero and steps up to protect Ava from her out-of-control ex.

Mason Gatlin (Nathanyael Grey) might genuinely be a goner since he seems like a great character to kill off.

No one will miss Mason, so GH writers might as well add him to the body count.

Now that Mason is spying on Ava, he’ll surely get on Ryan’s bad side. Ryan may stab Mason and turn his hook on Austin next.

Of course, we should mention that the anti-venom is in short supply at the hospital.

Elizabeth Baldwin (Rebecca Herbst) offered that hint, which obviously set up the possibility of the staff running out at a crucial time.

The hook drama isn’t over if that clue’s any indication.

It just might become Ryan’s weapon of choice in his next bloodbath, so others besides Mason and Austin could end up in grave danger.

Ryan will be a man on a mission when he makes his getaway from Spring Ridge.

There’ll be some scary moments for several Port Charles residents once Ryan is on the loose and ready to cause chaos.

General Hospital spoilers say Austin could become a patient at GH for a change, so stay tuned for updates on any injuries that might be looming.
