NGHTMRE Premieres Remix Of ZHU And Skrillexs Working For It

September 04, 2024
blog

Even though trap might not be the genre of choice for entry-level electronic music fans for much longer, there are still a handful of producers in the game who manage to make waves. In 2015, that ever-dwindling list of names surely includes NGHTMRE, and the remix of ZHU and Skrillex’s “Working For It” that he premiered at Echostage in Washington DC stands as proof.

Recommended Videos

In typical NGHTMRE fashion, the remix incorporates samples and effects that make for a somewhat theatrical iteration of the original track’s dissonant progressions. Visceral synth stabs at the drop make for an invigorating rise in the energy of the arrangement – or maybe it’s just that NGHTMRE made it a point to stand on top of the DJ table for the duration of this section.
