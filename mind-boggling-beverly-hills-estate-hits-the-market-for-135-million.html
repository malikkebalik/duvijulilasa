Mind-Boggling Beverly Hills Estate Hits The Market For $135 Million

July 29, 2024
blog

If you're unhappy with your current living arrangements and have always dreamed of living in the famed 90210 zip code, then I may have some very good news for you! Especially if you happen to be a billionaire with hundreds of millions of dollars to burn on your new home…

Earlier today it was revealed that an absolutely stunning Beverly Hills estate has hit the market.

The asking price? $135 million.

This 18,000 square foot home has 360 degree views of Los Angeles and sits on 2.5 acres of extremely private property. It has 8 bedrooms and 12 bathrooms and was previously owned by the late comedian Danny Thomas. Danny bought the house in the 1960s and built the original house in 1970. After the comedy legend died in 1990, his heirs (including daughter and actress Marlo Thomas) sold the property to the current owner for $15 million. The current owner reportedly spent millions on renovations.

So who is the current owner? Unfortunately, whoever currently owns the house has done an exceptionally good job of keeping their identity secret. The house is legally owned by an entity called Inch & Meter, Ltd which lists a lawyer named Michael Alan Grayson and presumably Michael's son Todd L. Grayson as the officers. There's practically no information on the internet about either lawyer or Inch & Meter. Hopefully we'll learn more in the coming months!
