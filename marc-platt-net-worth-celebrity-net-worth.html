What is Marc Platt's Net Worth?

Marc Platt is an American film, television, and theater producer who has a net worth of $20 million. Marc Platt was born in Pikesville, Maryland in March 1957. Platt graduated from the University of Pennsylvania and studied at New York University law school before he started working as an entertainment attorney. Platt first executive produced the movie Campus Man in 1987. He produced the films Josie and the Pussycats in 2001, Legally Blonde in 2001, Legally Blonde 2: Red, White & Blonde in 2003, Honey in 2003, Happy Endings in 2005, The Perfect Man in 2005, The Seeker: The Dark is Rising in 2007, Wanted in 2008, Rachel Getting Married in 2008, The Other Woman in 2009, Nine in 2009, Cop Out in 2010, Scott Pilgrim vs.

Platt also executive produced the TV series Taking the Stage from 2009 to 2010. He was nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award in 2005 and a BAFTA Award in 2012.
