What is Chris Jasper's Net Worth?

Chris Jasper is an American musician who has a net worth of $6 million dollars. Born in Cincinnati, Ohio on December 30, 1951, Chris Jasper is best known for being a member of the Isley Brothers as well as Isley-Jasper-Isley. He is currently a solo artist and record producer. He became active in the music industry in 1969 after meeting and jamming with neighbors Ernie and Marvin Isley. The formed the band known as The Isley Brothers, and Jasper's first studio appearance was on the album it's Our Thing.

Jasper left The Isley Brothers in 1984 to pursue the spin-off group Isley-Jasper-Isley for which he sang on the hit single, "Caravan of Love." The band eventually fizzled out, and Jasper began chasing a solo career in 1988. Jasper's expertise includes vocals, keyboards, and the Moog synthesizer. He currently runs his own New York-based record label known as Gold City Records. In 1992 The Isley Brothers were inducted to the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame. Jasper is currently married to his wife, Margie, and has three sons by the names of Michael, Nick, and Christopher.
